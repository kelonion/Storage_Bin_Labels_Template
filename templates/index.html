💡 Pro Printing Tips

  • Test print first on plain paper. Hold it over a label sheet to check alignment and orientation.
  • Match orientation: set your printer to Landscape for the landscape template, Portrait for the portrait one.
  • Set scaling to "Actual Size" or "100%" — do NOT use "Fit to Page" or the labels will be misaligned.
  • Load sheets one at a time if your printer tends to grab multiple sheets.
  • Use "Plain Paper" in your printer settings (not label or cardstock) for best feeding.
  • For laser printers: let the toner dry for a few seconds before handling.
