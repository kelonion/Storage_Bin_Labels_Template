💡 Pro Printing Tips

  • Always do a test print on plain paper first. Hold it over a label sheet to check alignment. Remember to set landscape orientation.
  • Set scaling to "Actual Size" or "100%" — do NOT use "Fit to Page" or the labels will be misaligned.
  • Load sheets one at a time if your printer tends to grab multiple sheets.
  • Use standard paper type in your printer settings (not label, not cardstock) for best feeding.
  • For laser printers: let the ink/toner dry for a few seconds before handling.
